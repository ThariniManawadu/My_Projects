Dine In

Smooth your sizzling nights with the hotel’s open air dining with the sound of the crashing waves, the fragrance of tropical trees and blossom. The dining area consists of incessant sea view directly which could make your dining extra special. Expect the unexpected from hotel chefs’ adventurous, attentive dishes together with both authentic Sri Lankan cuisines and tasteful western dishes for your tang together with Dining area entertainment will endow with seasonal bands of both western and classical

Tarshish Restaurant

World class cuisines, splendid view and elegant atmosphere at Tarshish Restaurant over look both sea as well as the swimming pool allowing you to take pleasure in your serving of food arranged by our endowed chefs’. Bistro offers variety of cuisines with special theme nights including Asian, Mediterranean, Sri Lankan specialties and much more…… Lobster and other Seafood specials can be ordered on our Sea view Terrace, with a special fine dining experience. Guests are welcome to enjoy the difference with Tarshish Restaurant which will tease your taste buds like none other.

Coral Bar

Quench your thirst with your favorite cocktails invented by our mixing artists to the tune of fascinating sunsets and frozen thrash. World renowned liquors and wines are also served every night for your necessitate. The perfect situate to enjoy the sunset and have a chit chat with your crowd while being paid pampered for the beats of crashing waves

Terrace – “The Deck”

Ideal location for a romantic candle lit dinner with your beloved or other special event while enjoy the magnificence of Indian Ocean, rolling waves and the silence atmosphere. Acquire comfort in your private moment while our staff serves your choice of menu. No matter whether to treat yourself or your loved ones the terrace is equipped with lavish set menus, Snacks and drinks for your preference
