Welcome to Serene Beach Hotel

Step in to a luxury charismatic holiday destination of Hikkaduwa

Imagine a luxury holiday destination with your family and friends around, Serene beach hotel the recently renovated tropical southern coast holiday destination just 90Kms away from Colombo city and 130 Kms from Bandaranayke International Airport. Envision amusement with your crowd facing unrivalled panoramic view of the sea directly from private balcony in your room or having the most famous sea bath, sun bath at tropical destination, Hikkaduwa.

Through unmatched level of unique hospitality and superior customer service from opening the doors, our team of connoisseur will assist you at all times to make sure you are been well taken care at our hotel. Inimitable Sri Lankan welcome and charm highly reflects at every corner of Hikkaduwa beach hotel with dedicated staff to make you smile with their quality of service..

With the just few steps away from the beach, our guests will enjoy the crashing waves and the sun set by just sitting at their private balcony, which will undeniably bring charming feel of relaxation and best of the holiday at this paradise island Sri Lanka with the best location for Coral reef, Diving , surfing, Madu Ganga, whale watching and many more to make your stay tremendous.
