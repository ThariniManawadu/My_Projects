Local Weddings

Make you beach wedding dream come true. Tie the knot in mesmerizing fashion with Hikkaduwa Beach Hotel, exchange vows or craft your Poruwa function with the backdrop of Blue Ocean, with the fine wishes of rolling waves. Talk to the hotel today with your exotic, fairytale wedding plan; select from Platinum, Silver or Gold packages and range of discerning menu options designed with many complimentary items / services for each package. The hotel has the facility of providing floral arrangements, Wedding cake preparation, blissful décor and even a photographer to capture those astonishing once in life time moments of both of you and your loved ones. Talk to us today for the best rate in town with dissimilar offerings as well as ambiance to enjoy your wedding ceremony to the utmost with your family and friends.

Foreign Weddings

We at The Hikkaduwa Beach Hotel are bound to fulfill your dream of a unique exotic destination beach wedding. Our dedicated team will work hand in hand with you to organize the ceremony, look after the legalities, prepare the decor, floral arrangements, wedding cake, photographs plus attend to other minute details needed to ensure that you and your partner will be the center of attraction and be the shimmering stars in your once in a lifetime experience as you celebrate your love with your friends and loved ones with a Sri Lankan flair, riding an elephant and traditional dancers included! Celebrate your love and exchange vows as one under the tropical sun and sandy beaches on endless beaches with the backdrop of turquoise blue waters of the Indian Ocean.
