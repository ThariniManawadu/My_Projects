Events

  • Hikkaduwa Beach Festival: This beach party full of DJs and music takes place in the month of October or December and lasts for 3 days.

  • Thai Pongal :This is a Hindu harvest festival held in honor of the sun god and is celebrated on January 14. Worship at the kevel is mandatory for people who follow this particular faith.

  • Madu Pongal: In rural areas, a sequel to Thai Pongal called Madu Pongal comes several days later.

  • Raid Amazons 2018, a Multi-Sport adventure event exclusive for daring adventurous females which has become a hall mark international event in tourism destinations. A large number of teams in smaller groups take part in a massive adventure challenge.

  • Kandy Esala Perahera 2018 (17th to 27th November 2018), The Esela Procession of Kandy is the ostentatious pageant of the Asia held annually to respect the sacred Tooth Relic of Lord Buddha which is honourably housed at the palace of Kandy called DaladaMaligawa whic epresenting a unique symbol of Sri Lanka.
  • Vishuddhi Haramba Martial Arts Show, Visit Kandy Annual cultural show promotional programme is organized by the Department of Trade, Commerce and Tourism Central Province to promote Central Province Tourism. Visit Kandy 2018 programme will be performed “Vishuddhi Haramba Martial Arts” show.

  • Getabaruwa Esala Perahara 2018, Getabaruwa Esala perahara 2018 will be held from 20th - 26th October 2018 at the Getabaru Rajamaha Vihara temple in Deniyaya.
