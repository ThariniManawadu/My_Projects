This is All About Serene Beach Hotel

Serene Beach Hotel provides room and suite accommodation intended to deliver modern trendy style with inspirational and relaxed stay for all guests. The hotel offers four exotic varieties of rooms and suites to suit your plan. Includes, Double Standard rooms designed with a private balcony and partial sea view from the room with brand new furniture for your well-appointed stay, Get a good night sleep at Hikkaduwa beach hotel with comfortable king size beds, Double Superior or deluxe rooms are offered with direct sea view and large windows to provide with uninterrupted sight. Family suite is Hikkaduwa beach hotels’ the most special offering with two double beds ,a living area for a chit chat and a hot tub (Jacuzzi)with a maximum occupancy of five guests.

With the just few steps away from the beach, our guests will enjoy the crashing waves and the sun set by just sitting at their private balcony, which will undeniably bring charming feel of relaxation and best of the holiday at this paradise island Sri Lanka with the best location for Coral reef, Diving , surfing, Madu Ganga, whale watching and many more to make your stay tremendous.

Step in today and sense the astonishing service at Serene Beach Hotel.
